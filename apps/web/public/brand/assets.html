Construction

Anatomy

tile
28 × 28, corner radius 8. Linear gradient at 135° (top-left → bottom-right) from #0D9488 to #14B8A6.
glyph
Pulse line on a 24 × 24 inner viewBox: M3 12h4l2-6 4 12 2-6h6. Drawn at 16 × 16 (≈ 6 px margin on every side). Stroke 2.4, round caps, round joins.
wordmark
Inter Tight 600, 17 px, letter-spacing -0.012em. .health shifts to Inter Tight 400 in muted neutral.
gap
10 px between mark and wordmark in the horizontal lockup.
min size
Mark-only: 16 px. Horizontal lockup: 24 px tall.
clear-space
Always reserve at least ½ × tile-height of empty space around the lockup. No surrounding text or graphics inside that area.

Mark · 3 variants

Square mark for favicons, app icons, avatars, and any context where the wordmark won't fit. Primary is the default; the other two cover monochrome print and dark backgrounds.

Primary (color) 28 × 28 Teal-gradient tile + white pulse. Use anywhere you have colour and the tile can show.
Single-ink black 28 × 28 Solid black tile, white pulse. For B&W print, fax, newsprint, embossing.
White knockout 28 × 28 White tile with pulse cut to transparent so the background shows through. For dark photographic backgrounds and video.

Horizontal lockup · 3 variants

Mark + "klinika.health". The default everywhere there's room — nav bars, email signatures, hero sections, app stores.

Primary (color) 148 × 28 Default lockup. Used in the marketing site nav, login pages, and the app topbar.
Single-ink black 148 × 28 Letterheads, mono printing, regulatory filings.
White knockout 148 × 28 Mark knocked out of solid white; wordmark in white with a translucent .health domain.

Utility · favicon, app icon, wordmark

Specialised assets for shipping the brand outside the product surface.

Favicon 32 × 32 Slightly heavier stroke (2.6) so the pulse survives at 16 px. Drop straight into <link rel="icon">.
App icon 1024 × 1024 For the App Store, Play Store, and PWA install. Tile fills the canvas — the host OS applies its own mask.
Wordmark only 106 × 20 Type-only logotype. Use when the mark already appears nearby in the layout.

Animated

Standalone SVGs with native SMIL animations — no CSS, no JS, no external dependencies. Loops forever (every 2 seconds) inside <img>, <object>, or inline. Use on the login splash, marketing hero, and onboarding intro.

Animated mark 28 × 28 Tile heartbeats while the pulse line continuously redraws. Loops forever.
Animated lockup 148 × 28 Mark heartbeats with the pulse-trace cycle while the wordmark sits steady. Loops forever — use sparingly.
