§1 · Authentication

MFA — email code on new devices

Every login from an unrecognized device asks for a 6-digit code sent to the user's email. Once verified with "mos pyet përsëri", the device is trusted for 30 days. Mirrors the visual language of the existing login screen — same logo placement, same field styling, same form scale.

3.5 Logout other sessions · confirm

Dilni nga sesionet e tjera?

Të gjitha sesionet e tjera përveç këtij do të mbyllen menjëherë. Pajisjet e besueshme mbeten të besueshme — hyrja tjetër nga to nuk do të kërkojë kod të ri.
Why two separate actions Hiq besimin revokes the device-trust cookie (next login from there needs MFA).
Dilni nga sesionet kills the auth token (current logged-in tab gets booted).
Doctors confuse these — keeping them visually distinct & phrased separately.

A single muted line beneath the existing autosave indicator on each visit, showing who last edited it and when. Clickable; opens the full change history modal for that visit. Appears only on edited visits — brand-new visits show nothing.
