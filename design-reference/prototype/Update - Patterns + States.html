§2 · DICOM viewing

Lightbox për imazhet e ultrazerit

Click any thumbnail in the visit's Ultrazeri panel to open. Full-screen dark overlay, image centered, ◀ ▶ arrows for multi-image studies, 1×/2× zoom, Esc to close. Patient + study date in the top-left so the doctor never mis-attributes a study.

Lidh studim ultrazeri

When auto-match by patient ID / DOB fails, the doctor picks manually from the 10 most recent unassigned studies received from the ultrasound machine. Each row shows when it arrived, a thumb strip, and image count.

§5 · Soft delete

Undo toast — "Vizita u fshi."

Dark pill at bottom-center after a destructive action. 30-second drain bar gives a clear deadline; "Anulo" rolls back. Slides up on appearance, down on dismissal. No spinner, no icon clutter.

§6 · Save failure

Ruajtja dështoi

Surfaces when auto-save retries are exhausted (typically network). Lists the unsaved fields with their current values so nothing is lost; "Ruaj lokalisht" stashes them in IndexedDB so a refresh won't kill the work.

§9 · Connection status

Treguesi i lidhjes — në cep të topbar-it

Invisible when online (subtle green dot, no label), amber + label when offline, pulsing dot during reconnect. Sits between the search and the user avatar so it shares the topbar's right-side rhythm.

Why amber, not red An offline state in a clinic is not a fault — auto-save buffers locally and resyncs when reconnect happens. Amber signals "attention", not "broken". Red is reserved for actual data-loss risk.
